Our Story

We are storytellers first, producing features, short films, and television series with the same adaptable creativity found in nature.

Who We Are

At Kaweah Creative, we embrace storytelling as nature embraces transformation. Our name embodies the mischievous, opportunistic spirit of the natural world - a 'winged intelligence' that knows no bounds. Like this spirit, our stories refuse to be confined by genre.

Just as a river carves its own path, we believe each narrative carries within it the seeds of its perfect expression. At Kaweah Creative, we're not just making films - we're capturing the ever-shifting patterns of human experience, one story at a time.

Our Leadership

Meet the visionary behind Kaweah Creative

Harris Kauffman

Founder & Creative Director

Harris Kauffman is a writer, producer, and the founder of Kaweah Creative. Shaped by early encounters with Kubrick's 'The Shining' and the short fiction of O'Connor and Proulx, he founded DEVOUR, a platform dedicated to narrative exploration. A UC Berkeley graduate, Kauffman began at Zoetrope: All-Story before transitioning to The Directors Bureau.

His producing credits include 'A Crooked Somebody,' 'Donny's Bar Mitzvah,' and the family adventure 'Treasure Trackers,' which he also wrote. A 2020 Sundance Institute Creative Producing Lab Fellow, he continues to merge literary sensibilities with visual storytelling at Kaweah Creative.
